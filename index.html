Northern Hospital is a key healthcare facility located in the Northern Province of Sri Lanka, serving communities with essential medical services. It plays a vital role in providing treatment, emergency care, and specialist consultations to people in the region. Equipped with trained medical staff and basic healthcare infrastructure, the hospital continues to support the well-being of residents, especially in remote and underserved areas.

This hospital serves over 700,000 patients annually. It handles more than 20,000 deliveries and conducts over 35,000 minor and major surgeries each year.
